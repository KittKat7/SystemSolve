As a technician navigating the data center's system, you find 
yourself at a critical juncture. The catastrophic failure has left your
communications with the central process sporadic at best. To ensure your 
safety and the integrity of the system, you must adhere to basic, 
singular commands. 

Navigate your character to the reboot point by issuing single commands 
("Up", "Down", "Left", or "Right").

Input one command at a time, and press the submit button to move in the 
respective direction. Continue issuing commands until you reach the 
reboot point, once you have the next level button will become available.
